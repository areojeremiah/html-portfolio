The Best Three Cartoons According to Me

My best three cartoons of all-time.

Tom and Jerry

This is my favorite out of the list. It is a comedy cartoon series of a cat (Tom) trying tirelessly to catch a mouse (Jerry). If you are wanting to have a good laugh, I'll recommend this for a start.


SpongeBobSquarePants

This is another cartoon series that evolkes a sense of nostalgia. My second favorite, this is one on a different plane entirely, filles with characters and various uniqueness. A yellow sponge, a starfish, a squirdward, a crab, a squirel, jelly fish and more fun characters all in a sea.


Pinocchio

Unlike the first two, this is not a series but it is also a great cartoon to see. This is about a strange magical talking wood that was spared by a puppet maker, privelegde to live like a real boy, he misused a number of the opportunities he had and he learnt his lessons. And at the end, he got want he always wantes.
